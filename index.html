Dear Xenia,

Happy one month! Even though we've only been together for 31 days, it's felt like forever. I guess that's because it's been longer than a month in a way, but that's a secret between us.

Apparently I sound like a broken record sometimes, but I can never express how happy you make me and all that you do for me. I didn't know just how much one person could do for me in all aspects of my life. You're my girlfriend, my best friend, a friendly acquaintance, and a whole lot more. You always drive me around, study with me, eat good food with me, and hang out with me every single second. Whenever I'm not with you, I'm always thinking about you: what you're doing, what you're thinking about, what I want to say to you next, and when the next time we'll see each other will be.

Even in this short amount of time, you have become such a sweet, relaxing, and important part of my days. You make the ordinary moments feel prettier, funnier, and warmer just by being there with me. The thing I thank you for most is how you're someone I can open up to and share my thoughts and feelings with. As you already know it's hard for me sometimes to say what I'm actually thinking and stuff from the past that affects me now. But you always sit there patiently, listening to me, understanding me, and making me feel validated. It's something I haven't experienced before, and something I'll always appreciate.

I made this little site as a small gift to you that won't fade away, die out, or get lost in a clutter. All you have to do is open up your phone and you can read this or any of the other things whenver you want. Thank you for your smile, your patience, your support, and most importantly, your love. You're my favorite girl in the world.

I hope this is only the first of many little milestones we get to celebrate together. One month down, but every time I see you, it still feels like day one. I love you Xenia!

From,
Jimmy
